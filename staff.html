Christine M. Calandra, head instructor and proprietor of Wildflowers Farm, specializes in Hunter Seat Equitation and Open Jumpers. She comes to us with 21 years' riding and showing experience, having successfully competed on the A-circuit in the Tri-State Area and on the intercollegiate circuit as well.

Christine is an ARIA Certified Level II Instructor in both Hunt Seat Equitation and Show Jumping, and a Level I Instructor in Side Saddle. She has over ten years of experience in instruction, and holds an Elementary Education Certificate in both Pennsylvania and New Jersey. She attained a B.A. from Muhlenberg College, and completed a minor in Equestrian Studies from Alfred University.

Bettie Greber is our facilities manager. With more than 20 years of horse ownership, she contributes her extensive knowledge of pasture, footing, small farm equipment, and water management. In addition, Bettie oversees our public relations and is available to give scheduled tours, primarily Tuesday through Sunday. She is also willing and able to assist during emergency vet and farrier calls. Her wonderful spirit and incredible organizational skills make her a most welcomed part of the Wildflowers' family.
