Wildflowers Farm is located on Cranbury Road, roughly equidistant from the townships of Princeton Junction and Plainsboro. A map of the location and directions are provided, however, do not hesitate to call if further directions are needed.

 

From Route 1: Turn south on Princeton-Hightstown Road (Route 514). Upon crossing over the railroad tracks, turn left at the light onto Route 615 and proceed on Cranbury Road about one mile. Farm entrance is on the left.

From the Turnpike: Take Exit 8 and follow the directions to Route 133, the Princeton by-pass. Upon reaching the terminus of the by-pass, make a right onto Hightstown Road and head toward Princeton Junction.

After ~2 miles, make a right onto Rabbit Hill Road and proceed until it intersects with Cranbury Road. The entrance to the farm will be directly ahead.
