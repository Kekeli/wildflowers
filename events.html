2008 Year End Results

Many congratulations are in order to members of the Wildflowers Farm show and pace teams and several of Hidden Creek’s Horses.

Eight of our riders attended the Turkey Trot Hunter Pace at the Horse Park of New Jersey. Congratulations to Liz Endres on Thelwell and Marilyn Can on Litey who placed 6th and Michelle McBride on PJ and Micaella Raglione on Kissy (despite a knee-injuring fall) for placing 7th in the Junior Division. Way to go to Krissi Ressler on Brego, Sue Sheerin on Paris, Lauren Casarona on Juan and Irmak Hatiboglu on Brandy for a fantastic pace! Lauren and Irmak ended 9th in time, while Sue and Krissi wound up 25th (out of 52 Open Teams).

The end of the Fall 2008 Season at The Meadows showed off the great show skills our riders have mastered this year and the superior instruction and coaching by our very own, Fran Przyblowski! Congratulations to Fran on Leading Trainer Fall 2008. Congratulations to Laura Foster on Rocky in the following divisions:

  • Year End Grand Champion Pre-Preliminary Jumpers
  • Year End Reserve Champion Working Hunter
  • Year End 3rd Junior Equitation

Congratulations to Mira Yuan on Thelwell in the Mini-Stirrup Division.
Congratulations to Chuck Cameron aboard HCF’s Vis a Vis in the following divisions:

  • 2008 Lynn Dominico Perpetual Trophy
  • Year End Reserve Champion Adult Long Stirrup
  • Year End 4th Adult Rider

Congratulations to Carly Riemann aboard HCF’S Vis a Vis in the following divisions:

Congratulations to Krissi Ressler and Grace Calandra on Thelwell in the Leadline Division.

Our horsies did fantastic too!!! Congratulations to Rocky with a 3rd place, Gold with a 4th, HCF’S Vis a vis in the Low Green Hunter division and HCF’s Bette in the English pleasure and Arabian Award.

That’s some list of awards, guys!!! Way to go also to all of our riders that tried showing or Hunter Pacing for the first time this season. It is my great pleasure to see not only piles of ribbons but more importantly the superior sportsmanship that all of our riders demonstrated. You all made us very proud!

Holiday Drill Team - Friday's 5pm to 6pm (that means mounted and warmed up at 5pm!). Performance is Dec 19th.

Cost is $150 per rider. Team Members are only permitted to miss 2 practices. Any material missed must be learned before the next practice. Each rider must be able to groom, tack, walk, and trot. There will be both a Mini-Stirrup and an Open Team. All interested riders should call, see, or email Christine.
